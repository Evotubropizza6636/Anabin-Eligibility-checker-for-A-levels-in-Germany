Verify Your Eligibility Manually

\

You can determine your eligibility yourself by visiting the following website: https://anabin.kmk.org/db/schulabschluesse-mit-hochschulzugang# and selecting 'Gro\'dfbritannien', followed by 'International Advanced Level'.

\

Alternatively, if the link is invalid or fails to open, you can go directly to the Anabin homepage. Navigate to the 'Schulabschl\'fcsse mit Hochschulzugang' (School leaving certificates with university entrance qualifications) section, select 'Gro\'dfbritannien', and then choose 'International Advanced Level'.

\ \

Important Disclaimer

\

It is still highly advised to check all entry requirements with your chosen university before submitting an application[cite: 1]. This tool provides information for guidance only, as each university retains the right to set its own specific entry requirements, including subject choices, combinations, and language prerequisites, within the broader recommendations of the Central Office for Foreign Education[cite: 1].

\

As a best practice, if you are not absolutely sure about your eligibility, please contact your university's admissions office, use the uni-assist contact form available on their website, reach out via the contact information provided by Anabin, or consult any other concerned authority involved in your admission process.
