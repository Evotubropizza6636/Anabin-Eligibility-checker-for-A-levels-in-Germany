You need to determine your subject-specific eligibility from this matrix as well. Absolute confirmation of your intended program to be studied at a university following within a specific subject zone (for example, whether studying the program "International Engineering" at an XYZ public university following Anabin regulations falls under Mathematical or Technical, or under Natural Sciences specialization) can only be estimated or inquired directly at the university, uni-assist, or Anabin. Verify Your Eligibility Manually

You can determine your eligibility yourself by visiting the following website: https://anabin.kmk.org/db/schulabschluesse-mit-hochschulzugang# and selecting 'Großbritannien', followed by 'International Advanced Level'.

Alternatively, if the link is invalid or fails to open, you can go directly to the Anabin homepage. Navigate to the 'Schulabschlüsse mit Hochschulzugang' (School leaving certificates with university entrance qualifications) section, select 'Großbritannien', and then choose 'International Advanced Level'.

Important Disclaimer

It is still highly advised to check all entry requirements with your chosen university before submitting an application. This tool provides information for guidance only, as each university retains the right to set its own specific entry requirements, including subject choices, combinations, and language prerequisites, within the broader recommendations of the Central Office for Foreign Education. As a best practice, if you are not absolutely sure about your eligibility, please contact your university's admissions office, use the uni-assist contact form available on their website, reach out via the contact information provided by Anabin, or consult any other concerned authority involved in your admission process.
